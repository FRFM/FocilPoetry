Just Going Backwards
So please do not hurt me
Because I am afraid of you
Go away as far as you can
So , do not trust me when I say
I always want you in my back
And the truth is that
I need to lie about how I feel
I see you and
I do not know how a man could fall in love
With a woman as you
Now, I am bemused about the order this should be
Is the only thing that I could think
Without you
This life would be easier
And the following is just a fib
I could say that I love you
